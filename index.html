About

Building, shipping, and explaining what I learn.

Nowak Apps is a one-person studio. I design and build the apps myself — usually in Kotlin and Jetpack Compose — and I share what I learn through conference talks, meetups and internal sessions. If you're organising a conference or want to chat about an app, I'd love to hear from you.

What I work on

  • Mobile apps. Android-first, Kotlin / Compose, occasionally KMP. Habit2Goal is the current flagship.
  • Refactoring & architecture. Real-world strategies for taming long-lived codebases without losing momentum.
  • Kotlin internals. Coroutines, Flow operators, interop with Java — making the abstract concrete.
  • AI tooling. Hands-on with Copilot CLI, Claude Code, custom agents, skills and MCP servers.
