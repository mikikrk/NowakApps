Frequently Asked Questions

When will the app be available?

Public store links will appear here once review passes. Current build targets Android & iOS.

Does it require an account?

No account required. For the best privacy all data is stored locally on the device. There are tools to transfer current state from device to device on the device migration.

Will my data sync across devices?

No, in order to grant the best privacy for your habits, they are stored solely on you device. There is though an option to export them to file and import it on another device in case you'd like to migrate your data.

Is there a dark/light mode?

Yes. Automatic based on system preference with accessible contrast ratios.
