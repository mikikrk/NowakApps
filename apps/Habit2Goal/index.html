Features

Built for people who are going somewhere — not just staying busy.

Today, with a goal in view

Your active goal sits at the top of the home screen with a real-time progress ring and on-pace forecast. Below it, today’s habits — tap + to claim points if you succeed with a habit, − to remove when you feel you have failed in it. The whole routine fits on one thumb-reachable screen.

  • Live progress ring per active goal
  • On-pace days-to-completion forecast
  • Each habit can give you both positive and negative points — you choose

Per-goal deep stats

Each goal earns its own progress ring, activity timeline, consistency report, active rate, best-month and forecast. The kind of depth that makes progress feel tangible.

  • Progress ring + on-pace forecast
  • Cross-habit contribution breakdown
  • Activity timeline + consistency score

Your year, painted in dots

Every habit gets its own calendar — month grid for context, full-year view to step back and see the pattern. Green dots are wins, orange dots are slips. The picture tells you the truth long before any single day does.

  • Month grid with per-day point totals
  • Full-year overview at a glance
  • Tap any month to drill down into a day

A history worth scrolling through

Every goal you’ve ever completed lives in Goal History — cover photo, completion date, full contribution data. See exactly which habits paid for each milestone. On hard days, this is the screen that reminds you why you started.

  • Permanent record of completed goals
  • Cover photos + completion dates
  • Re-open any goal to revisit the data

FAQ

Quick questions, honest answers.

Is it free, or do I have to subscribe?

Free forever: one active habit, your active goal, and the essential daily stats.

Premium unlocks unlimited habits and advanced insights, deeper history, and expanded breakdowns for a clearer view of what’s working.

Wait — does the app actually give me money?

No. Habit2Goal is a self-reward concept. You decide what each goal “costs” in points and what real-world reward unlocks when you hit it. We just keep the books honest so you don’t cheat past-you out of the win.

How is this different from a streak tracker?

Streaks tell you if you showed up. Habit2Goal tells you what your showing up is buying you. Each habit has a weighted point value; each goal has a price. Your discipline compounds into named, real outcomes — not a number that only matters to your phone.

Can I track habits I want to avoid?

Yes. Negative habits (impulse buying, skipping workouts, late-night doomscroll) can be assigned negative point values. Slip up and your goal gets further away. Honest accountability beats vibes.
