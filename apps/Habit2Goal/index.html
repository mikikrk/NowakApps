From intention to execution

Habit2Goal bridges the gap between what you plan and what you actually do. By tightly integrating habit loops with long‑term targets, it enables meaningful progress without overwhelm.

  • Clarity: see exactly what moves each goal forward.
  • Focus: highlight the next impactful action.
  • Motivation: visual momentum & completion feedback.
  • Insight: granular stats per day, habit & goal.
  • Adaptation: iterate goals as habits evolve.
