Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Habit2Goal is designed as a local‑first habit & goal tracking application. This policy explains what (minimal) data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data Storage

All habit, goal and statistics data you enter is stored locally on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers because we do not operate any backend for core features.

2. Accounts

No account or sign‑in is required. Future optional sync features (if released) will be opt‑in and covered by an updated version of this policy.

3. Analytics & Tracking

The app currently does not include third‑party analytics SDKs. If lightweight privacy‑respecting analytics are introduced later, you will be able to opt out.

4. Permissions

Only essential platform permissions are requested. The app does not access contacts, location, microphone, or photos unless a feature explicitly needs it and you grant consent.

5. Third‑Party Services

App store distribution (Google Play / Apple App Store) may collect anonymized metrics per their own policies.

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you are a guardian and believe data was provided by a child, contact us for deletion steps.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. A future in‑app data export / wipe tool is planned.

8. Changes

Policy updates will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Email: contact@example.com
